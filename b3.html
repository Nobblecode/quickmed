QUICKMED: A NEW APP TO IMPROVE ACCESS TO HEALTHCARE IN NIGERIA

QuickMed is a new app that aims to solve the problem of primary health care delivery in Nigeria. The app allows users to order an ambulance, access medical infrastructure, and get advice from medical personnel directly from their smartphones. -How it works QuickMed is a simple and easy-to-use app. To use the app, users simply need to create an account and provide some basic information about themselves, such as their name, age, and location. Once they have created an account, users can then use the app to order an ambulance, access medical infrastructure, and get advice from medical personnel. -Order an ambulance If a user needs an ambulance, they can simply open the QuickMed app and select the "Order an Ambulance" option. The app will then automatically locate the nearest ambulance and dispatch it to the user's location. -Access medical infrastructure QuickMed also allows users to access medical infrastructure, such as hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. To do this, users simply need to open the QuickMed app and select the "Find a Medical Facility" option. The app will then display a list of all the medical facilities in the user's area. -Get advice from medical personnel QuickMed also allows users to get advice from medical personnel directly from their smartphones. To do this, users simply need to open the QuickMed app and select the "Talk to a Doctor" option. The app will then connect the user with a doctor who is available to provide advice. -Convenience and accessibility QuickMed is a convenient and accessible way to get the medical care you need. The app is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and it can be used anywhere in Nigeria. QuickMed is also affordable, and it is a great option for people who do not have health insurance. -Medical Insurance and Loans QuickMed also offers medical insurance and loans to its users. Medical insurance can help to protect you from the financial burden of medical expenses, and medical loans can help you to pay for medical care that is not covered by insurance. Conclusion QuickMed is a new app that aims to solve the problem of primary health care delivery in Nigeria. The app is convenient, accessible, and affordable, and it offers a variety of features that can help you to get the medical care you need. If you are looking for a way to improve your access to healthcare, then QuickMed is the app for you.

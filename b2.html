QUICKMED HELPS VOLLEYBALL PLAYERS STAY HEALTHY AND SAFE

QuickMed Provides Medical Support at Beach Volleyball Conference Tour 2023 QuickMed, a subsidiary of Adain Technology, provided medical support at the Beach Volleyball Conference Tour 2023, which was held from April 14-19 at Jabi Lake Park in Abuja, Nigeria. The QuickMed team was led by Mr. Godwin O.G., a company associate, and consisted of four staff members: Mr. Lawal Quazeem, Miss Jan Deborah, and Miss Adakole Catherine. The QuickMed team was on-site for the entire duration of the event and provided medical care to a total of 23 volleyballers. The most common injuries treated were ankle twists, arm and finger locking, muscle pulls, scratches, and headaches. The QuickMed team was well-received by the event organizers and participants. The president of the federation, Engr. Musa Nimrod, appreciated the team's work and assured them of support during and after the event. The technical director of the event, Mr. Meladin, also commended the team's selfless services. The QuickMed team's participation in the Beach Volleyball Conference Tour 2023 was a success. They provided valuable medical care to the participants and raised awareness of their company and services. The team is looking forward to working with the Nigeria Volleyball Federation again in the future. Here are some of the key takeaways from the event: • QuickMed was able to provide timely and effective medical care to the participants of the Beach Volleyball Conference Tour 2023. • The QuickMed team was well-received by the event organizers and participants. • QuickMed was able to raise awareness of its company and services through its participation in the event. • QuickMed is looking forward to working with the Nigeria Volleyball Federation again in the future. If you are looking for a reliable and affordable quick medical response system provider, QuickMed is a great option. The company has a team of experienced and qualified professionals partners who are committed to providing quality care. QuickMed also offers a variety of services, including on-site medical care, Ambulances services, and Quick call.

QuickMeds is an Emergency Medical & Response Services (EMRS) platform that connects users to an array of quick medical services

  • 1. Health facility location.
    2. Emergency ambulance services.
    3. Health and medical personnel.
    4. Other Emergency services powered by the Government and
    5. Access to medication and dosing information.

We provide service that is targeted at connecting users to quick medical response service within societal divides. As a result of poor service delivery experienced by these persons in emergency and to reduce mortality rate of patients QuickMed was created to cater for these persons’ needs. QuickMed also seeks to provide a platform where those with vehicles and are available to use their vehicles can become Private Ambulance Service Providers to foster the mantra, “Save a Life First”.

1. To connect users seamlessely to service providers and enable a conducive virtual working environment for quality service delivery.
2. Excellence service delivery system.
3. To address the challange of pre-hospital emergency service.
4. To enable unhindered access to registered medical facilities and services.
5. To be a one stop private access to emergency services providers.
6. To build a synergic partnership with service providers within the medical emergency industry and the technological landscape.
7. To deliver quick and reliable response ambulance service.

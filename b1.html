Quickmed Lunches Medical Delivery App

One of Nigeria’s foremost Medical Services Companies, QuickMed Emergency Services, have announced the official launch of her mobile application, QuickMed Pro. The medical service application which became available for download since Friday, 18th November 2022 on the google play store, is absolutely free. The QuickMed Pro App is an emergency medical service delivery application that enables users to connect with medical professionals, medical laboratories, and emergency agencies. The users after downloading can reach out to medical service providers, hospitals and medical laboratories. According to QuickMed, the innovative application, also provides ambulance transport for users of the app to the nearest medical facility. It also have an e-consultation service on the platform for those needing medical advice. According to the company, the idea came in October 2021 with the aim to solving life threatening issues through technology. Solving problems with science and technology. This is because there is lack of proper medical response system, no high way response system even home/community response system which has taken a lot of lives. It explains, further, “theme of discussion amongst stakeholders in the medical, emergency agencies, and technology field is” The Future of Technology and Emergency Response Services in the Society.” ” The world is right in our hands with the tap of our screen, and an in-depth discourse will be held on how we can leverage technology within our local space to save lives within the shortest time possible thereby reducing the mortality rate daily. QuickMed Emergency Services is a subsidiary of Adain Technologies. QuickMed is an innovative emergency service delivery platform with the vision of saving lives first and fast. With its collaborative partnership with medical facilities and medical professionals, the use of the app serves to make a difference as it would aid in a mortality rate reduction.
